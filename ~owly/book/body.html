Crucial Interventions: An Illustrated Treatise on the Principles & Practice of Nineteenth-Century Surgery

Very grizzly book showing medical illustrations with little to noting hidden. Lots of great looking drawings of guts and gore, so much fun!

Great book for anyone interested in medical history, one of the only downsides is that it can cost a bit, with my hardback copy costing me just under £20, oh, and also the woman at the shop where I got the book from said something on the lines of "Looks like you're going to be on a list" - hahaha, piss off :-)

By: Ritchard Barnett
Year: 2015
ISBN: 978-0-500-51810-6
