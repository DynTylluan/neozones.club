My Favourite Texts, Reports, and Stories

Here on this page are a bunch of links to texts, reports, and (you guessed it) stories that I have enjoyed reading throughout the years for a number of reasons. The main reason why I may enjoy some text is mostly going because it is about tech being used in a positive way, another is if it could have only been written then and there, such as with "Protect and Survive", that is about what to do if a nuclear weapon has been used and the threat of fallout; very 1980s.

Other times, I enjoy reading texts that are off-the-wall crazy, such as with Sylvia Driskell vs Homosexuals, who wanted to sue every homosexual for being gay.

For much more text, please visit Bytemoth's Bold type page, for texts written by other people.
