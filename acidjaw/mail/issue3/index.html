MINCED OATH
From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A minced oath is a euphemistic expression formed
by misspelling, mispronouncing, or replacing a
part of a profane, blasphemous, or taboo term to
reduce the original term's objectionable
characteristics. Some examples include "gosh"
(for God), "crumbs" (for Christ), "crikey" (for
Christ kill me), "darn" or "dang" (for damn),
"doggone" or "gosh darn it" (for goddamn), "heck"
(for hell), "gee", "jeez", or "Jiminy Cricket"
(for Jesus Christ), "feck","fudge", "frick",
"fork", flip or "eff" (for fuck), "coot" (for
cunt), "sharlookey", "shoot", "shivers", "sweet",
"shucks", or "sugar" (for shit), and "bushwa"
(for bullshit).
