>WHY, HELLO THERE 👋
Hello there, friends, welcome to issue 17B of
AcidMail, still one of the worst things to have
ever been made (yes, I know that you hate it
here, I hate it here also, but we might not need
to sit through this for very long).

This is a very special issue of AciMail made by
just Cass "Owly" Python as an attempt at trying
to get H Y P E around the possibility of
continuing AcidMail, but look -- we're jumping
the gun here a little bit, aren't we? So, what
*is* an AcidMail?

First started in 2020, AcidMail was the name of a
newsletter co-written by people that would be
published weekly on the 'net for the viewing
"pleasure" of you (yes, YOU!), the idea of the
newsletter was that it was not going to be like
most of the newsletters that you might subscribe
to as we have attitude, yo, and we will take open
jabs at the reader (we love you really XOXOXO)
while trying to stay light-hearted and trying to
ignore the, quite frankly, bleak news of the
world that was going on at the time.

The newsletter could be quite experimental at
times, such as with issue 6 that was mostly
written by Talk to Transformer, a neural network
that was made to try and complete text - another
example of noticeable things that we did was with
issue 13 where we got our readers to make
whatever they wanted to do in JS Paint, what was
at the time a fairly new webapp made to run like
MS Paint.

When the newsletter was first started, NeoZones
was quite the small community, being mostly made
up of people who were being hosted on the site,
however, since the newsletter stopped, a lot has
changed and we really have gotten out sticky
fingers in a lot of pies, and because of this, I
think that it could be worth a shot to try and
resurrect this newsletter. If you like the look
of this newsletter and think that you can help
with it, such as writing something for it or even
suggesting a link,
join https://neozones.club/discord

For a possible test run, we hope to show you what
AcidMail can be and we (uh, well *I*) hope that
you enjoy it a lot.

: EVIL BAT BOI
: https://www.furaffinity.net/view/43624024
: https://wiki.neozones.club/index.php?title=File:batboi.png
After not using MS Paint (or even anything to do
with Microsoft) for, God, over five
months, I, Cass Python, decided that I would draw
Xania the Linux fox girl, but it quickly turned
into me drawing an almost furry version of
Dracula, so I thought what the hell? Let's draw
Dracula, after all, they *were* a furry!

I was originally going to only draw this in
black, white, and red, but I thought it would be
fun if I were to make it so the only colors* that
I could use would be the ones found on the ZX
Spectrum, a British home computer
(https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Zx-colors.png).

It's a little funky, but you know what, we need a
little funk in our life... or undead life! (wait,
are vampires undead??) Can you just imagine
someone walking down the street in these colors?
My sweet Lord, it would stand out.

As the colors are a little... strange... I
welcome you to feel free and edit around this
drawing and see if you can make the colors from
the ZX work better, or Hell, make some art USING
the colors, that would be really cool to see!

*I am sorry to my fellow Brits on here, but I use
American English online... I have failed you all.
