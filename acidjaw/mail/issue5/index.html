:THIS SITE IS DEDICATED TO VELMA DINKLEY
:http://www.velmadinkley.com/
Seemly a whole site dedicated to "most
under-appreciated member of Mysteries Inc", that
being Velma Dinkley. This is a really odd
experiences, as this site's contents was last
updated in 2017, so you think "oh, this is a joke
to make a website look like it's old", but no, if
you chuck the URL in the Internet Archive, you
will see that it has been going since 2002. A
whole website dedicated to Velma Dinkley from
Scooby Doo since 2002. This is insanity.

There's a fanfic part to the site, there's an art
page, even a "Velma Chat", an IRC-like room where
you can "chat with other fans of Velma!" - ???

Honestly, you kind of need to give some credit to
the webmaster, keeping this site up since 2002 is
quite something, and most people probably would
not be able to keep a site updated for so many
years.
